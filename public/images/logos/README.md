# Company Logos

Add company logo files here in PNG format:

- `timely.png` - Timely logo (preferably horizontal/wordmark version)
- `jernia.png` - Jernia AS logo
- `komplett.png` - Komplett Group logo

## Logo Guidelines

- **Format**: PNG with transparent background preferred
- **Height**: Logos will display at 24px height, width auto-scales
- **Style**: Use official company logos, preferably horizontal wordmark versions
- **Color**: Full color logos preferred, will match the professional design

## Where to Find Logos

1. Company websites (usually in press/media kit sections)
2. Official brand guidelines
3. LinkedIn company pages
4. Request from company communications teams
